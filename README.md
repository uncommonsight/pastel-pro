# Pastel You

A simple, beautiful photo editor for adding soft pastel tones to your images. Pick a preset, adjust the intensity, and save. That’s it.

-----

## Presets

### Pastels

Soft, vibrant pastel color presets with a warm film feel.

|Preset      |Description              |
|------------|-------------------------|
|Original    |No filter                |
|Blush       |Warm rosy pink           |
|Dusty Rose  |Deeper, moodier pink     |
|Peach       |Warm peachy glow         |
|Mist        |Cool grey-blue atmosphere|
|Lavender    |Soft purple dream        |
|Sage        |Muted earthy green       |
|Cream       |Warm buttery light       |
|Sky         |Light open blue          |
|Apricot     |Deep orange warmth       |
|Petal       |Romantic pink-lilac      |
|Cotton Candy|Dreamy pink-purple swirl |

### Gradients

Top-down color washes that kiss the sky of your image.

|Preset     |Description                 |
|-----------|----------------------------|
|Original   |No filter                   |
|Golden Hour|Warm amber wash from above  |
|Blue Hour  |Cool deep blue dusk feel    |
|Rose Sky   |Soft pink blush from the top|

-----
