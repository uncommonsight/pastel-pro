# Pastel You

A mobile-first photo editor built for iPhone. Pick a preset, fine-tune with manual controls, crop and straighten, then save. No account, no ads, no cloud — just your photo and a clean UI.

Install it from Safari: tap Share → Add to Home Screen.

---

## Presets

Presets are organized into folders. Tap a folder to browse, tap a preset to apply, tap the active preset (or the name in the header) to remove it. The **Strength** slider appears below your photo when a preset is active.

### Gradients

A color wash painted over your image — great for sky shots. Tap a gradient preset, then use the **rotate button** (top-left corner of the photo) to cycle the wash direction: top, left, bottom, or right. The rotate button only appears when the Gradients folder is open and a gradient is active — open the editor or crop tool and it steps aside until you're done.

## Manual Adjustments

Tap the sliders icon to open the editor. Adjustments are additive on top of any active preset. Double-tap a slider to reset it to zero.

**White Balance**
- Warmth — shift the temperature warmer or cooler

**Light**
- Exposure — overall brightness
- Highlights — brighten or recover bright areas
- Shadows — lift or deepen dark areas
- Whites — fine control over the brightest tones
- Blacks — fine control over the darkest tones

**Detail**
- Contrast — punch or flatten the tonal range
- Fade — lift the shadows for a film-faded look
- Saturation — overall color intensity

**Color**
- Per-channel saturation for Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Cyan, Blue, Purple

## Crop & Straighten

Tap the crop icon to enter crop mode.

- **Drag corners** to resize the crop box
- **Drag inside the box** to pan the image
- **Straighten slider** — rotate up to ±45°
- **Auto** — detects the horizon and suggests a correction; tap again to undo it
- **Lock icon** — locks the current aspect ratio while resizing
- Undo in crop mode only affects the crop and rotation — it does not touch your preset or manual adjustments

---

## Compare

Hold your finger on the photo at any time to preview the original image. Release to restore the edited version.

---

## Saving

Tap **Save** to export. On iPhone this opens the native share sheet — save to Photos, AirDrop, or share anywhere. Output is PNG at up to 4096px on the long side.
